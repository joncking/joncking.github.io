This study demonstrated that machine learning techniques can decode naturalistic video content from the brains of domestic dogs. Decoders based on the actions in the videos were successful, with 90 minutes of fMRI data obtained from each dog across three sessions.

*Acknowledged contributor* -- recognized for contributions to the machine learning methodology.
